# World Expansion Workflow

Use this reference to add characters, locations, factions, rules, or a new arc
to an existing world.

## Procedure

1. Audit the target with `diagnose`, `card-audit`, `lore-audit`, and `recall`.
2. Separate reusable source material from world-local runtime state.
3. Present a concise expansion plan and obtain confirmation.
4. Import new cards into the reusable library without `--new-world`.
5. Attach cards to the intended world through the supported event or CLI.
6. Add durable setting facts as worldbook entries with deliberate constant or
   triggered behavior.
7. Update the Persona only when the user's world-local identity changed.
8. Preserve the current opening and story unless replacement was requested.
9. Verify the target world and confirm no unrelated world changed.

## Ownership

- Physical traits, identity, voice, and stable motivations belong to the role.
- Places, organizations, rules, history, and public events belong to lore.
- The user's identity belongs to Persona.
- Played events and temporary state belong to the story ledger.

## Pitfalls

- Do not create temporary worlds while importing supporting cards.
- Do not edit reusable library cards for story-local evolution.
- Do not duplicate character facts into lore.
- Do not delete files to repair normal workflow output.
